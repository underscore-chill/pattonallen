Meet the Owner

Robert Jeffreys, owner of LEXI GRAY PROPERTY MANAGEMENT, uses his unique skills and backgrounds to create a truly new kind of management company. Over the last several decades, Robert has built and grown The LEXI GRAY Team, one of the area's top performing real estate teams, with a vast network of repeat clients in and around the Goldsboro area.

Robert has spent his career analyzing and creating products out of real estate data, and has worked directly with some of the largest insurance companies, hedge funds, and property management companies in the US. Robert Jeffreys brings these skills, along with his track record of success, to LEXI GRAY PROPERTY MANAGEMENT.
